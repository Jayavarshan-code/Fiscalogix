## 🏗️ Q1: THE REAL-TIME SPINE (Infrastructure Hardening)
**Objective**: Transition from "Static Polling" to a "Real-Time Push" global nervous system.

### Technical Milestones:
1.  **Event Mesh Deployment**: Launch **Redpanda/Kafka** as the central message bus.
2.  **The 13-Pillar Unification**: Refactor all pillars into the **Common Data Protocol (CDP)**. Every pillar (Risk, EFI, Docs) now speaks a unified JSON/Protobuf language.
3.  **Latency Benchmark**: Implement sub-500ms propagation from the "Edge" (Telemetry) to the "Brain" (Consensus).

**✅ Success Metric**: 100% of global risk updates are reflected in the terminal in under 5 seconds (Target: <500ms).

**✅ Success Metric**: Reduction of "Total Clicks to Decision" from 15 (Legacy) to 3 (Fiscalogix).

**Objective**: Move the heavy-lifting simulation math into **Tensor-Native Hardware**.
