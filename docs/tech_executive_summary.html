What It Provides
🧠 AI Financial Digital Twin
8-stage event-driven pipeline (DataIngestion → MLInference → CLV → Decision → SituationAssessment → DispatchPlanning → AgentExecution → Persistence). Each stage is fault-isolated — a failure in stage N never aborts stage N+1.
⚠️ Dual-Model Risk Engine
XGBoost classifier (tabular risk features) fused with a PyTorch Graph Neural Network that propagates contagion across the supply chain graph. SHAP explainability on every prediction. GNN blend decays with graph-hop distance — no false alarms on distant nodes.
🇮🇳 India GST Compliance Engine
Quantifies IGST working capital drag per shipment (ITC recovery lag × WACC). Computes DGFT duty drawback receivable. Auto-generates GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B. Flags exporters not on LUT. Reconciles ITC before filing, not after penalty.
🛣️ Geopolitical Route Optimizer
NetworkX directed graph over ports, hubs and warehouses. Dijkstra with a composite weight function: base_cost × (1 + risk × beta). Detects active strikes, territory types, and customs delays. Multi-modal: Ocean / Rail / Truck.
📊 Monte Carlo Financial Simulation
1,000-cycle VaR simulation with hard-capped Pareto distributions (max 180-day delay — based on worst historical events). ReVM formula: contribution_profit minus risk, time, FX, SLA, tariff and GST costs. Liquidity scoring + cashflow shock detection.
💬 RAG AI Copilot + Agent Mesh
sentence-transformers (all-MiniLM-L6-v2, 384d) + pgvector for semantic search over contracts, invoices and financial history. Claude (Anthropic) as reasoning layer. 5 autonomous agents: Risk, Financial, Routing, Anomaly, Executive.

What Makes It Unique
FIRST GST ITC lag quantified per shipment. No other tool computes the WACC-adjusted working capital cost of ITC recovery delay on Indian imports — it's invisible in every P&L today.
UNIQUE GNN contagion propagation. Risk doesn't stop at one port — the graph model propagates disruption probability across the supply network with hop-distance decay.
FIXED Bounded Monte Carlo. Competitors use uncapped Pareto distributions producing nonsensical VaR outputs. Fiscalogix caps at 180-day maximum — credible numbers CFOs actually trust.
BUILT-IN Industry-aware seasonality. Demand models carry separate seasonal profiles per vertical (FMCG, Automotive, Pharma, Electronics) — not a single B2C retail multiplier applied to everyone.
AUDIT Full decision traceability. Every pipeline stage emits a StageOutput with timing, input hash, and result. Auditors can reconstruct exactly why any decision was made.
